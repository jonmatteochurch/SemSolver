Detailed Description

template<class X>
class SemSolver::PSLG< X >

Class for handing Planar Straight Line Graphs.

By definition, a PSLG is just a list of vertices and segments. It can also contain information about holes and concavities, as well as regional attributes and constraints on the areas of triangles.
